## Overview

**What are free models?**
Free models on OpenRouter are AI models that can be used without any per-token charges. They are typically:
- Hosted by the model providers at no cost to users
- May have usage limits or rate constraints
- Perfect for development, experimentation, and low-volume production use
- Accessible via the same OpenRouter API as paid models

**Why use free models?**
- **Cost-free experimentation**: Test different models without financial commitment
- **Learning and development**: Build and prototype without API costs
- **Proof of concept**: Validate your application before upgrading to paid models
- **Access to cutting-edge models**: Many free models are state-of-the-art

**Important notes**:
- Free models often have lower rate limits than paid models
- Availability may change as providers adjust their offerings
- Check OpenRouter's current status page for real-time availability

## Important Considerations

### Rate Limits
Free models typically have stricter rate limits than paid models. Check OpenRouter's current limits before building production applications.

### Model Experimentation
Always test multiple models for your specific use case. The "best" model depends on your particular needs - quality, speed, cost (free), language, and domain specificity.

### Monitoring Availability
Free model availability can change. Providers may:
- Discontinue free hosting
- Move models to paid tiers
- Introduce new free models

Stay updated by checking OpenRouter's model directory regularly.

### API Usage
All these models are accessed through the same OpenRouter API:

```bash
curl https://openrouter.ai/api/v1/chat/completions \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "HTTP-Referer: YOUR_SITE_URL" \
  -H "X-Title: YOUR_APP_NAME" \
  -d '{
    "model": "stepfun/step-3.5-flash:free",
    "messages": [
      {"role": "user", "content": "Hello!"}
    ]
  }'
```

Replace `YOUR_API_KEY` with your OpenRouter API key and the model ID with any of the free models listed above.
